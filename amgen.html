    • Syringe Needle Measurement System - In partnership with Amgen

    Fluid dynamics, Arduino, SOLIDWORKS, soldering

    Problem Statement

    Many of Amgen’s drugs are self-delivered. Painful delivery can deter patients from self-administering these drugs at home and is determined by pressure drop in the needle, most greatly affected by the needle’s internal diameter (ID). The goal is to develop a measurement system that can determine the effective ID of each needle coming out of manufacturing before they are sent off to be used with patients at home.

    Our Solution

    To combat this issue, my team and I designed a solution which relies on a gravity-driven linear bearing and fluid dynamics calculations. Our design uses an automated timing system composed of a loadcell start trigger and button stop trigger. The time taken for a 3mL syringe with a needle gauge of choice to fully dispense via a constant pressure from the linear bearing is recorded. This time is then used as an input to the Hagen-Poisseuille equation to return the effective internal diameter of the needle.
