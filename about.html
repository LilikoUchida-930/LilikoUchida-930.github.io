    • My name is Liliko Uchida (legally spelled "Ririko" due to Japanese phonetics). I grew up in Northborough Massachusetts, where music, singing, cross country, and track consumed a large portion of my life. During my second half of high-school, I fell in love with physics and knew I wanted to carry that passion along with my constant desire for a creative outlet to college with me. Based on this, I decided to go to school for mechanical engineering.

    At Tufts, one of my largest engagements has been working with the Student Accessibility and Academic Resources (StAAR) Center as a subject tutor. I love to help others learn. Particularly, I have made a personal commitment to give back to the physics community as I have benefitted greatly from the generosity of peers and incredibly motivating and inspiring mentors.

    I love to experience various creative outlets during my free time including design (which recently sparked my interest in learning some web development on the side), music (check out my music page!), and writing. I also admire the outdoors, and have included some photos on this page of favorite places I've been too!
