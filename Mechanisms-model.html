    • Mechanisms Model

    Laser cutting, Relative velocities, Angular motion, Translational motion

    For this project, I decided to model the velocity of slider B as it relates to theta. The image to the left shows my diagram of the mechanism along with assumptions, trigonometric relationships of the system, and calculations, ultimately resulting in an expression for the velocity of the slider in terms of theta.

    For a high-fidelity prototype, I lasercut the mechanism model with clear acrylic and assembled it using steel pins.
