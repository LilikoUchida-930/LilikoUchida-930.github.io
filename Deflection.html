    FEA in SOLIDWORKS, Castigliano's Theorem



    The goal was to design an aluminum structure that would deflect 0.5in under a 200lb load and have a spring constant of 400. Finite element analysis was paired with a mathematical validation using the strain-energy method (Castigliano's Theorem) during the design process. The final results of testing are shown below.
