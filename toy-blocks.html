    LabVIEW, Motors

    The objective of this project was to create a smart toy for kids. To begin, my team and I went to the local preschool to interview a group of kids on their dream toy. As the final consensus, we decided to make something which allowed the children both autonomy and creativity (which they expressed as a desire in much simpler terms during our mini interviews).

    Our final product was a set of motorized blocks which could be connected together and controlled via a mobile app.
