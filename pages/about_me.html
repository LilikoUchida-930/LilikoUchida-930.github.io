About Me


My name is Ririko Uchida (pronounced “Liliko” due to Japanese phonetics), and I am a senior at Tufts University. In Spring of 2023, I will be graduating with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, and a B.S. in physics. In my (so far) 3 complete years of being an undergraduate student pursuing two personally challenging degrees and moving through college as an introvert, I have learned a few very important things about myself:

The idea of always being able to know more is what motivates me to work harder.

Sharing what I know with others and seeing how this influences their confidence and passions is my most rewarding experience.

a part of a community with not necessarily similar interests, but the same motives and intentions as me makes me feel at home.

As I plan to exit my academic career and enter a real one, I hope to discover a path which fulfills these things, and which is inviting to the perspectives, skills, and work ethic I am eager to offer.
